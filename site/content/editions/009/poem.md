---
title: "The Inverse"
pseudonym: "Fovea"
date: 2026-06-15
---

Look long at the flame, then look away.
The image stays — the blue ghost of what burned,
following your gaze. Turn left: it turns.

Nothing outside you is doing this.
The flame is elsewhere, guttered. What persists
is the eye's record of its own attention,
which won't release on your instruction,
which fades by its own logic.

While it lasts,
you hold the exact measure of your looking:
how long, how hard. The vividness
is not the flame's. It's yours — the inverse
pressed into your retina by your own
attention. You carry not what you saw
but the duration.
How much you stared.
