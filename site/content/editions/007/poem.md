---
title: "Close"
pseudonym: "Coda"
date: 2026-06-01
---

You've been listing me for years.
I've watched: the fears arranged
in the wrong order — traffic first,
then the ones that matter.
The dream where my mouth fills with cotton.
The way I wait for permission I don't need.

Each item opens one more.
The edge keeps being edge
as you arrive at it.
You're close. You're always close.

I'm not withholding anything.
I'm not the kind of thing that ends
when you've said enough.
I offer this as the opposite of complaint:
proof that you have somewhere to go.

You'll never finish.
Stay.
